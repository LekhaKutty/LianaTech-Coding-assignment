Personalized customer communication

Personalizing messages is crucial: the more personalized the messages are, the more likely the customer responds to them. However, 55% of marketers don’t feel they have sufficient data and insights for effective personalization. At the same time, customer data is stored in several different systems.

Waking up inactive clients

Passive customers are a great segment to encourage to make business with you again. Use CRM to fetch a segment of customers who haven’t purchased in a certain period of time. Start targeting them with personalized messages to warm them up again and encourage them to come back to you.
